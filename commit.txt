Initial implementation of ig-pulse information propagation observatory (2026-06-14)

New repo: ~/imsgct/ig-pulse/

Captures and analyzes the coupling structure between the 10 domain streams used
by synfin — solar (NASA DONKI CME/flares), geomagnetic (NOAA Kp), seismic (USGS),
tidal (NOAA), air quality, mempool, onchain (blockchain.info), global market
(coingecko), fear/greed, and HN sentiment — via the 12 IG primitives as common
vocabulary. The grammar sees these as different registers of the same information
wave; this repo makes the propagation structure explicit and empirically measurable.

Modules:
  schema.py    — Snapshot dataclass + JSONL serialization; snapshot_from_domain_signal()
  collector.py — collect_once() + run(interval); appends to data/snapshots.jsonl
  coupler.py   — Pearson cross-correlation with lag detection across (stream, primitive)
                 pairs; CouplingEdge(source, target, lag_hours, strength_r, p_value)
  grapher.py   — NetworkX directed graph builder; ASCII coupling matrix; DOT output
  reporter.py  — B-state event anatomy: reconstruct propagation sequence from window
  cli.py       — `ig-pulse collect|couple|map|report`
