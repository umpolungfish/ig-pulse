ig-pulse: SIC-POVM density matrix framework + new streams — 2026-06-27

The 36-stream observatory is a d=12 heteroskedastic POVM approximation.
Full SIC geometry requires 144 = 12² Weyl-Heisenberg elements. Coverage: 24/144 (16.7%).

Stream 34 — wiki_entropy (Ř recognition + Γ granularity)
  Shannon entropy of Wikipedia top-1000 daily article view distribution
  Low entropy = attention spike (election, disaster, discovery) → Ř alert
  Also emits wiki_concentration = 100 - entropy% as Γ signal
  TTL: 6h (daily top-1000 changes slowly)

Stream 35 — bgp_routing (Γ granularity + Þ topology)
  RIPE NCC RIS: total unique ASNs in global IPv4 routing table (~87k)
  Rapid change signals internet fragmentation or major routing event
  Emits bgp_asns (Γ scaled) + bgp_delta (Þ %change)
  TTL: 4h

Stream 36 — arxiv_ai (Ř recognition + ⊙ criticality)
  ArXiv cs.AI + cs.LG daily submission count via Atom feed totalResults
  High count → recognition event (community responding to major release)
  Rate alert on count; spike alert on delta vs. previous poll
  TTL: 12h
