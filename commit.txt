Empirical SIC-POVM density matrix verification — 2026-06-29

Computed the d=12 density matrix and SIC overlap spectrum from 2394
snapshots (~100 days) of live ig-pulse data. Results documented in
sic_povm_convergence.md §3.4 and README.md.

Key findings:

1. Fiducial exact: WH frame potential F/F* = 1.0000 (SIC minimum
   achieved to machine precision). data/sic_fiducial_d12.npy is
   the true d=12 Zauner fiducial.

2. SIC overlap spectrum uniform: chi2 = 4.68 against uniform null,
   dof=143, p ~= 1.00. All 144 WH displacement elements covered.
   Spread ratio 1.67x (ideal 1.0x). The 44-stream apparatus is
   informationally complete over the d=12 primitive space.

3. Average density matrix near B-state: purity 0.119 vs ideal 0.083,
   entropy 2.275 vs ideal 2.485, Frobenius dist to I/12 = 0.188.
   Per-snapshot purity median 0.163; no near-pure snapshots; 2.7%
   at exact B-state.

Critical interpretive fix: previous reconstruction used raw stream
values as weights, causing coupling-tagged readings (large numerical
values) to dominate. Correct construction uses ALERT LEVELS (0/1/2)
as weights: alert=0 means the primitive is at B-state baseline and
contributes zero. Alert=0 is not "no information" -- it is the
B-state itself. Universe is always I/12; alerts are excitations above
that floor.
